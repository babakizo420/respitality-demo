Support that fits real life.

Respitality provides respite and community-based support for individuals with developmental disabilities and the families who care for them. We focus on everyday support - helping individuals stay engaged and supported, while giving caregivers time to rest, attend to responsibilities, or recharge.

Explore Support Our Approach

We are a small service by design, allowing us to listen closely and shape support around real needs.

Who We Support

We support individuals with developmental disabilities and the families and caregivers who support them. Our services are best suited for those who benefit from one-on-one or small-scale support, at home or in the community.

Our Services

We provide thoughtful, real-world support tailored to your unique requirements.

Respite Support

Support that allows caregivers to step away while their loved one is supported with patience and respect, either at home or through supervised community activities.

Community Support

Assistance with community participation, supported outings, and everyday activities that build confidence and connection in real-world settings.

Skill Development

Where appropriate, support focused on building practical skills that encourage independence and future opportunities, particularly for employment readiness.

How Support Begins

Support begins with a conversation. Families or caregivers reach out, and we take time to understand needs, routines, and comfort levels before discussing next steps. The process is informal, thoughtful, and paced carefully.

Our Approach

  • We value dignity, privacy, and clear communication.
  • Respitality operates as a program by Alternatives.
  • We share a commitment to accountability, inclusion, and respectful, person-centred support.

Let's talk about what support looks like for you.

If you're exploring support options or have questions, we welcome you to reach out. We aim to respond within 1-2 business days and treat every inquiry with care and confidentiality.
